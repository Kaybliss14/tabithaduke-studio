About Tabithaduke

An expert in 3D modeling, built on years of practice.

An expert in 3D modeling with several years of experience, which has helped perfect these skills in the field. Tabithaduke has successfully created high quality custom VRChat avatars while working with various groups that deal majorly with characters — including comic and metaverse characters. That experience covers VRChat avatars, VTubers, custom avatars, and more.

Every avatar is built using Blender and Unity, with an ongoing focus on refining skills to deliver impeccable quality at a very reasonable price — to the point of being able to make a living from this craft. Feel free to reach out with any questions or commission requests.
